    About Us

    MOBO EVO (acronym of MObile roBOtic EVOlution) is the Middle-Size League Wheeled Soccer Robot team of the Yogyakarta State of University
    (UNY Robotics Team / UKM Rekayasa Teknologi / Engineering Faculty), Indonesia. We involve people working on several areas, contributing to the development of all the components of the robot, hardware to software. The MOBO EVO team started officially in 2010 with Fire Fighting Wheeled Robot, and in 2017 we started developed Middle-Size League Wheeled Soccer Robot. We are a team of college students working on this team like it's our full-time life. We are a big family. Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is much appreciated.

    Coach

    The Coach himself would be in charge of leading the assembly process of the robot that will participate in competition.

    Electronic

    This division performs its duties on the electronic part of the robot. They assemble electronic components that will support the robot working system.

    Program

    This division is in charge of create the program that run on the robot according to the wanted command.

    Mechanic

    This division is in charge of design the robot and also make sure the design built as good as expected.

    Official

    This division regulates all the records, correspondence, sponsorship, and so the others.

    MOBO EVO is one of Universitas Negeri Yogyakarta (UNY) Robotic team, focusing on autonomous soccer wheeled robots. MOBO EVO has competed in the fire-fighting and soccer robotic league since 2010. The team consists of students of Universitas Negeri Yogyakarta.
