About Us

MOBO EVO (acronym of MObile roBOtic EVOlution) is the Middle-Size League Wheeled Soccer Robot team of
the Yogyakarta State of Univeristy (UNY Robotics Team / UKM Rekayasa Teknologi / Engineering Faculty), Indonesia.

We involves people working on several areas, contributing for the development of all the components of the robot, hardware to software.
The MOBO EVO team started officially in 2010 with Fire Fighting Wheeled Robot,
and in 2017 we started developed Middle-Size League Wheeled Soccer Robot.

We are a team of college students working on this team like it's our full time life. We are a big family.
Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated.
